"Ephemeral"

This illustration project includes a comic cover and an interior page, both exploring the theme of impermanence. Created entirely in black and white, the absence of color emphasizes contrast, mood, and the fleeting nature of the moments portrayed.

Made in Clip Studio Paint, the work uses minimalism and negative space to reflect the delicate, quiet beauty of temporary experiences. The comic page continues the tone set by the cover, offering a subtle, introspective glimpse into a moment that doesn’t last—but lingers.
