"Dreamy Squirrel Flyer"

This flyer was created for a whimsical bakery concept called Dreamy Squirrel. The design focuses on evoking a cozy, magical atmosphere that reflects the bakery’s playful brand identity. Soft pastel colors, hand-drawn elements, and charming typography were used to convey warmth and sweetness—just like the treats the bakery would offer.

The flyer was designed to catch the viewer’s attention while maintaining clarity and friendliness, striking a balance between visual delight and functional communication.
